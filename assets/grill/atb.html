Achari tikka botti

Marinate chicken cubes with lemon juice , salt, ginger garlic paste and National Chilli Powder and leave for at least 20 minutes Heat ghee in a pan and roast the chickpeas flour and keep aside In another bowl add yogurt. Add in roasted chickpeas flour, salt and National Crushed Pickle. Mix well Add in marinated chicken and mix well In a pan add 3tbsp oil. Shallow fry the marinated chicken on slow flame till the chicken is tender Serve with onion rings, cucumber, french fries and raita
